Sunspots and Melanoma

AsymptoticPDC.get_sunspot_melanoma_data — Function
get_sunspot_melanoma_data()

This data is from Andrews and Herzberg. D. F. Andrews, A. M. Herzberg. (1985) Data: A Collection of Problems from Many Fields for the Student and Research Worker. Springer, New York.

u[:, 1]: Year 1936-1972 u[:, 2]: Annual male melanoma incidence (age-adjusted per 10e5) in Connecticut u[:, 3]: Annual total melanoma incidence (age-adjusted per 10e5) in Connecticut u[:, 4]: Annual sunspot relative number
